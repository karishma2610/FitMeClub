ABOUT US

We strive to provide the greatest tools, a wide variety of classes, competent personnel, and individualised guidance in a friendly setting. We are aware of how crucial creating a positive culture is. We believe in encouraging, supporting, challenging, learning and growing to be the best!  We are an ambitious firm and are always searching for exceptional people to join our team, from personal trainers, group fitness instructors, club managers, to our front-of-house employees. For those who are interested in working with us, please know that we are an open-minded organisation. We're committed to training, so you'll be urged to sharpen your already acquired abilities as you learn new ones. Contact us at fitmeclub@gmail.com.
